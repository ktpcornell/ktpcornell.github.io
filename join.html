Frequently Asked Questions

Anyone is allowed to rush — we gladly accept (and encourage) rushees from all disciplines! The only requirement is that you must have at least 3 semesters left in school after your pledging semester.

We are looking for students who are passionate about technology, eager to learn and grow, and committed to fostering community and collaboration.

Members gain access to mentorship, technical workshops, networking opportunities, social events, and a lifelong professional support system.

We have members from Computer Science, ECE, Information Science, Design, Economics, and more. Anyone passionate about tech is welcome!

We host retreats, game nights, formals, group dinners, and more. Our events help members bond beyond academics and projects.

Pledging is a manageable commitment designed to fit into a student’s schedule. Expect to dedicate a few hours each week for meetings, events, and bonding activities.
